TextFromImage — Privacy policy

Summary: OCR runs on your device. We do not operate a backend or receive your images or extracted text.

What is processed

When you upload, paste, or select an image from a web page, the extension runs local OCR (Tesseract) in your browser. Extracted text stays on your device unless you copy or download it.

Web images

If you use the context menu on a page image, the extension may request permission for that site’s origin to fetch the image bytes. No data is sent to AI4Context servers.

Permissions

  • storage — UI language and OCR language preference.
  • contextMenus — “Extract text from image” on images.
  • optional host permissions — fetch image from the page when you use the context menu.
